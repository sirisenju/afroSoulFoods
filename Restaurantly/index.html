About

Our Story

Afrosoul is a connection to the uniqueness of African culinary tradition that ignites the heart. Chef Efe was born into a family of crop and fish farmers, owing to his love of cooking and inspired by the vibrant African food scene in his mother's kitchen. Growing up around organic and fresh food is a privilege that helps to keep one's originality for natural, healthy, and tasteful meal. Chef Efe's passion for food preparation and cooking grew over the years, and he has made a career out of it. He has acquired training, worked with top professional international and local chefs in the hospitality sector, and holds diplomas in professional cookery and kitchen management, food safety, and science. In 2022 Afrosoul Connect Food Services was founded to serve, connect, and express his personal touch and the delights of African food heritage with a global fusion.
